Elvar Companion Extension Installation Guide
==========================================

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc.).
2. Go to the Extensions page:
   - Chrome: chrome://extensions/
   - Edge: edge://extensions/
3. Enable "Developer mode" (usually a toggle in the top right corner).
4. Click the "Load unpacked" button.
5. Select this folder ("extension").
6. The Elvar Companion extension is now installed!
7. Pin it to your toolbar for quick access.
